Founder

Rishabh Sharma

Rishabh Sharma (Developer RS / RS5) is a 16‑year‑old self‑taught full‑stack and cloud developer from Sikar, Rajasthan and founder of Zenuxs. He focuses on building scalable, production‑ready systems and has over 3 years of development experience.

He specializes in web development, backend infrastructure, DevOps and developer platforms. Through Zenuxs he builds authentication systems, developer tools, CLI utilities and open‑source software designed for real‑world production environments.

With more than 20 completed projects, he actively shares technical insights and developer humor on Instagram while maintaining open‑source projects on GitHub.
